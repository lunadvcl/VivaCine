Pirates of the Caribbean

Description of the film

Pirates of the Caribbean is an American fantasy supernatural swashbuckler film series produced by Jerry Bruckheimer, originating from a theme park ride by Walt Disney. It features the adventures of Captain Jack Sparrow and other iconic characters.
