La Casa de Papel

Description of the film

La Casa de Papel or Money Heist is a Spanish crime drama series developed by Alex Pina, which tells the story of two ingeniously thought-out heists. The first of them takes place within the walls of the Royal Mint of Spain, and the second - in the building of the Bank of Spain itself.
